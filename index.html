RESPLICE
Computational tools for analyzing RESPLICE RNA-seq data. Scripts and pipelines for processing, aligning, and quantifying splicing patterns from the RESPLICE (RNA-guided trans-splicing with CRISPR) experimental system.
github.com/hsulab-arc/RESPLICE →
Detecting Riboswitches with Diffusion + SOFM
MIT 9.530 project combining generative and unsupervised learning to identify riboswitches from RNA sequences. Uses a diffusion model for structural predictions, graph embeddings, self-organizing maps to capture conformational diversity, and a classifier to distinguish genuine riboswitches from random sequences.
github.com/cyrustau21/murfalo_cyrustau_switch_project →
